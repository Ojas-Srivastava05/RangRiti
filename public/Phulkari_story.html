Phulkari: Flowers in Thread

Step into the vibrant world of Punjab, where every stitch blooms with color, tradition, and the stories of generations.

Embroidered Stories

The Bridal Bagh

The Bridal Bagh is the crowning glory of Phulkari, created lovingly by mothers and grandmothers for a bride’s trousseau, symbolizing blessings, joy, and prosperity.

Heirloom of Blessings

Each Bagh is a labor of love, with every motif and color chosen to convey wishes for happiness and abundance. The embroidery covers the entire fabric, making it a dazzling display of skill and devotion.

Symbol of Status

Traditionally, a bride’s Bagh was a mark of her family’s pride and artistry, worn during weddings and special occasions as a cherished heirloom.

Generations of Artisans

Generations of Artisans have preserved Phulkari, with women across Punjab passing down stitches, stories, and motifs through the ages.

Learning by Heart

Phulkari is taught from mother to daughter, with each family and region developing its own patterns and color palettes. The craft binds communities and celebrates shared heritage.

Living Tradition

Despite changing times, Phulkari endures as a symbol of Punjabi identity and the resilience of its artisans.

Motifs of Phulkari

Motifs of Phulkari are inspired by nature and daily life—flowers, birds, geometric shapes—each carrying meanings of happiness, fertility, and protection.

Vibrant Imagery

Bright silk threads are embroidered on coarse cotton, creating shimmering patterns that catch the light. Popular motifs include the lotus, parrot, and wheat stalks, each telling a story of hope and abundance.

Symbolic Language

Every motif is more than decoration; it is a visual blessing, woven with intention and meaning.

Living Heritage

Living Heritage celebrates the ongoing evolution of Phulkari, blending traditional motifs with modern designs for a new generation.

Revival and Innovation

Contemporary artisans experiment with colors, fabrics, and products, from dupattas to home décor, ensuring Phulkari’s relevance and beauty endure.

Threads of Identity

Phulkari remains a vibrant symbol of Punjab’s cultural spirit—a living art that continues to inspire, empower, and connect.
