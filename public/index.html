Sacred Art Forms

Pattachitra

Ancient cloth scrolls telling epic tales of Lord Jagannath and Krishna, painted with natural pigments on handwoven canvas by master artisans.

Odisha • Puri

Kathakali

Classical dance-drama where gods and demons come alive through elaborate masks, intricate movements, and centuries-old storytelling traditions.

Kerala • Malabar

Gond Tribal Art

Indigenous forest art celebrating the sacred connection between humans and nature, painted with natural pigments and filled with spiritual symbols.

Madhya Pradesh • Tribal

Chhau Dance

A martial arts-inspired dance form where performers wear elaborate masks to portray mythological characters in dynamic movements.

West Bengal • Purulia
