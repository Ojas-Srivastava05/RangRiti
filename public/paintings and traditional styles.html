Madhubani

Characterized by its eye-catching geometrical patterns, this art form is known for representing ritual content for particular occasions, creating a visual narrative that speaks to the soul.

Folk Art

Warli

Utilizing a set of basic geometric shapes: a circle, a triangle, and a square, these paintings depict scenes of social life with remarkable simplicity and profound meaning.

Maharashtra, India
Scroll Art

Pattachitra

A traditional, cloth-based scroll painting, known for its intricate details and mythological narratives and folktales that bring ancient stories to vivid life.

Odisha, India
Natural Dyes

Kalamkari

This art involves hand-painting or block-printing on cotton textile, using a tamarind pen and natural dyes, creating masterpieces that celebrate nature's palette.

Andhra Pradesh, India
Narrative

Phad

A scroll-based Rajasthani folk art used to narrate epic tales of local deities through bold lines and natural pigments, preserving oral traditions in visual form.

Rajasthan, India
Gold Leaf

Tanjore Painting

Classical South Indian paintings known for their rich colors, surface richness, and compact composition with gold foil embellishments that shimmer with divine radiance.
